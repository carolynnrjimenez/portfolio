About Me

Hi, I’m Carolynn — a mathematics educator and curriculum designer interested in how people learn, reason, and develop identity through mathematics and computer science. I enjoy building projects that make complex ideas more accessible while maintaining rigor and care.

I have experience teaching secondary mathematics, designing curriculum, and supporting educators through coaching and program management. My background also includes data analysis and working at the intersection of education, technology, and equity.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading, hiking, and spending time at baseball games. I’m currently focused on building a small portfolio of curriculum and data projects, preparing for graduate study in mathematics education, and training for a half marathon.
