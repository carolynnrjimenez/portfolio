About Me

Hello! I’m a Mathematics Educator. I enjoy building things that help people learn, connect, and grow. I have been teaching at the Drew School for four years and serve as the 10th Grade Chair. I also have experience in program management and data analysis. This summer, I will serve as an instructional coach with Breakthrough San Francisco.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and hiking. My goals for this summer are to apply to graduate school to study mathematics education, build three projects, and learn a new language. I am also hoping to train for a half marathon and go to lots of baseball games to enjoy the sun.
